About us

  • Purpose

    The purpose of this website is to enable users to access, query, compare, and spatially visualize general plans in cities and counties across California.

  • Instructions

    Enter words separated by a comma in the textbox or a single phrase for exact phrase search. Click Search to query across the plans. The list of word occurrences in each plan will be shown. Click on the city of choice to view the PDF with the highlighted keywords. Plans were added in 2020, and we are in the process of adding updates. If you have any feedback please provide it here (Feedback).
